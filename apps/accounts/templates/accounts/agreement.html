User agreement

Confidentiality and responsible use.

This agreement applies to every account created on the platform. It defines how users should protect credentials, use the service responsibly, and avoid exposing confidential or personal data.

Acceptable Use

  • You must provide accurate registration information and keep your account credentials secure.
  • You must not use the service for unlawful access, scraping, fraud, abuse, or unauthorized data collection.

Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure

  • You agree not to disclose, share, publish, sell, copy, or distribute confidential, private, personal, or system-sensitive information obtained through the platform without authorization.
  • You must not expose other users' data, authentication material, internal API responses, or administrative information to third parties.
  • If you become aware of a security issue or unintended data exposure, you must report it promptly and must not exploit it further.

Security Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for maintaining a strong password and protecting your login credentials.
  • You must log out from shared devices and avoid sharing tokens or account access with others.
Why this exists The agreement makes confidentiality obligations explicit and gives you a clear compliance point to mention in project reviews.
When it is accepted A user must accept these terms during registration before the account is created.
What is stored The project records whether the user accepted the agreement and when that acceptance happened.
By creating an account, the user confirms they have read and accepted this agreement, including the confidentiality and non-disclosure obligations.
